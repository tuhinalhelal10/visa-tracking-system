The “Data Protection Information” document needs to be printed and submitted as a part of the Application file.

Prior to applying for your visa, be sure that you have checked all required documents depending on your purpose of travel.

Overview

We encourage you to use the online services provided.

You may create your account and after you receive a verification email, continue by setting a password. Now you are ready to login (please click Sign In on the right top corner of the Home page). On first login the system will ask you to select a Visa Application Center for Greece (VAC) based on your residence address.
